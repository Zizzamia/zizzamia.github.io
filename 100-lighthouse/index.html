Coinbase Lighthouse Score in May 2019

  • Where did we stand now?
  • We went from 20s to 8.5s TTI, and FCP from 8.5s to 1.7s.
  • We are still far from 100 score
  • But are we measuring the truth performance for all our users?
  • How can we be sure that the result we perform on a test machine is it the same all around the world?
  • There are two different enviroments to measure performance.
  • We have a controlled enviroment in the Lab, and is nice because we can collect so much data. Something like Chrome Dev Tools, WebPageTest or Lighthouse.
  • But it's not the real world and it's only a part of the truth.
  • Performance in the Field anything can happen, it's a lot different.
  • But Again, one way to see the difference between the two is: - the lab is like unit test - the field is like functional test
